
# Miro Storyboard — Copy Notes

> Paste these bullets into your Miro board as stickies to accelerate the storyboard.

## Act 1 — Problem Setup
- Who is the primary user? (persona)
- Context and trigger
- Pain framed with data ("today, this costs us X")

## Act 2 — Journey & Friction
- Current steps (1→N) with quotes
- Where time/quality is lost
- What "good" looks like

## Act 3 — Solution Sketch (MVP)
- KeyCommit concept in one frame
- What the user does
- What they get back (deterministic summary)

## Act 4 — Outcome
- Before/after metrics (lead time, accuracy, rework)
- Next bets after MVP
